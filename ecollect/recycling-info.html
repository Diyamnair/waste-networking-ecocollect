Recycling Guidelines

 

Please follow these guidelines to ensure that your recyclables are collected:

 
       
  • Rinse all containers before recycling
    •    
  • Remove lids, labels, and caps from containers
    •    
  • Flatten cardboard and paper products
    •    
  • Do not bag recyclables in plastic bags
    •  
 

Recycling Tips

 

Here are some tips to help you recycle more effectively:

 
       
  • Reduce your use of single-use plastics
    •    
  • Reuse bags, containers, and water bottles
    •    
  • Recycle as much as possible, including paper, plastic, glass, and metal
    •    
  • Participate in community recycling programs
